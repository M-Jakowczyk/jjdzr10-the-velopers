  "I Know" is a trivia game application where users can test their knowledge in various categories such as history, science, literature, and more.
  Players are presented with a series of multiple-choice questions and have to select the correct answer within a set time limit. The app keeps track of the player's score and progress, and provides feedback on areas where the player can improve their knowledge. With a clean and intuitive interface, "I Know" is a fun and engaging way to challenge oneself and learn new things.
